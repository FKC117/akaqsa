Our Islamic Mission & Educational Vision

Discover the foundation of authentic Islamic education that has guided Darsgah Kids for over 15 years, where every child's spiritual and academic journey is nurtured with wisdom, compassion, and excellence.

The Beginning of Our Journey

In 2009, a group of dedicated Muslim educators and parents came together with a shared vision: to create an educational institution where Islamic values would not be an afterthought, but the very foundation upon which all learning is built.

Founded by Dr. Amina Hassan, a scholar of Islamic education with over 20 years of experience, Darsgah Kids was born from the recognition that Muslim children needed more than weekend Islamic school. They needed a comprehensive educational environment where their Islamic identity could flourish alongside academic excellence.

Our Mission

"To nurture confident, knowledgeable, and spiritually-grounded Muslim children who embody Islamic values while excelling academically, preparing them to be positive contributors to both their communities and the world at large."

Islamic Curriculum Framework

Discover how Quranic teachings, prophetic traditions, and Islamic values are seamlessly integrated throughout all subjects

Holistic Integration Approach

Our curriculum doesn't treat Islamic education as a separate subject, but as the foundation that enriches every aspect of learning. From mathematics lessons that explore Islamic geometric patterns to science classes that marvel at Allah's creation, every subject becomes an opportunity for spiritual growth.

Core Islamic Studies

Quran & Tajweed

Daily recitation, memorization, and understanding of Quranic verses with proper pronunciation

Hadith & Sunnah

Study of prophetic traditions and their application in daily life

Islamic Jurisprudence (Fiqh)

Age-appropriate understanding of Islamic law and practical worship

Islamic Integration Across Subjects

Mathematics

Islamic geometric patterns, Zakat calculations, and mathematical concepts in Quran

Science

Exploring Allah's creation, scientific miracles in Quran, and environmental stewardship

History

Islamic civilization, prophetic biography, and contributions of Muslim scholars

Language Arts

Islamic literature, Arabic language integration, and moral storytelling

Community Values & Code of Conduct

Our shared commitment to Islamic values creates a nurturing environment where every member of our community can thrive

Compassion (Rahma)

Following the prophetic example of kindness and mercy in all our interactions

Honesty (Sidq)

Truthfulness in speech and action, building trust within our community

Respect (Ihtiram)

Honoring the dignity of every individual as part of Allah's creation

Excellence (Ihsan)

Striving for the highest standards in worship, learning, and character

Student Expectations

  • Begin each day with Bismillah and maintain Islamic etiquette
  • Show respect to teachers, peers, and all community members
  • Participate actively in daily prayers and Islamic activities
  • Maintain modest dress code in accordance with Islamic guidelines
  • Strive for academic excellence while helping classmates succeed

Family Partnership

  • Support Islamic education goals at home and school
  • Maintain open communication with teachers and administration
  • Participate in school events and community activities
  • Reinforce Islamic values and teachings at home
  • Volunteer time and skills to benefit the school community

Staff Commitment

  • Embody Islamic character and serve as positive role models
  • Integrate Islamic values throughout all subject areas
  • Provide individualized attention to each student's growth
  • Maintain professional development in Islamic education
  • Foster a safe, inclusive, and nurturing learning environment
